If you want to use Nanashino-chan music in YouTube videos, brand videos, client work, product demos, paid ads, social campaigns, or commercial content, choose a license that matches the intended use.

Platform Music Is Not a Full Commercial License

A track may appear inside Instagram, TikTok, YouTube Shorts, or another platform music interface, but that does not automatically make it cleared for monetized videos, sponsored content, client work, paid ads, brand campaigns, or broader commercial use.

Platform-library or credit-based usage may be suitable for non-commercial or small-scale projects, but it should not be treated as a substitute for a commercial license, sync license, paid advertising license, client-work license, or brand campaign license.
