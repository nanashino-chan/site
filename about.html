Origin and mission

Music designed to support the moment behind the screen

Nanashino-chan began YouTube activity in May 2024 and expanded into official digital music distribution in February 2025. The project grew from a simple idea: background music can be quiet without being anonymous, and useful without losing its emotional identity.

The sound is inspired by Tokyo at night—quiet streets, late trains, soft lights, cafés, desks, rain, and the space between daily life and focused creative work.

Rather than competing with narration, captions, product visuals, or storytelling, the music is intended to sit behind them. Soft drums, warm keys, understated melodies, and controlled arrangements help establish atmosphere while leaving space for the main message.

This approach connects two parts of the project: music for everyday listening and music that can function naturally inside videos, digital media, brand content, and other visual formats.

制作思想 Nanashino-chanが目指しているのは、強く主張して映像を支配する音楽ではなく、映像や言葉の背後で空気感、余韻、感情のトーンを支える音楽です。東京の夜や静かな日常をモチーフに、聴くための音楽と、映像の中で機能する音楽の両立を目指しています。
Independent, but informed by music-industry experience

The project is independently operated from Tokyo by a creator with prior experience working in the record industry and current hands-on experience across music production, digital distribution, rights management, creator-platform operations, and web publishing.

The music

Calm, urban, warm, and recognizable

The Nanashino-chan catalog moves across lo-fi hip hop, chillhop, jazzhop, calm instrumental music, chillwave, and Tokyo-inspired night sounds. The common thread is a low-distraction atmosphere with enough melody and texture to remain memorable.

Some tracks lean toward warm piano and jazz harmony. Others use city-night synths, relaxed grooves, soft percussion, or nostalgic textures. This range allows the catalog to support focus, reading, late-night work, quiet listening, and many forms of visual storytelling.

Discovery

Listener interest that goes beyond passive playback

Nanashino-chan music has passed 3,500 Shazam searches. A Shazam search begins when someone hears a track, becomes curious about it, and actively tries to identify the music.

That makes Shazam different from an automatic impression or passive stream. It provides a useful signal that the sound was noticeable enough for a listener to take another step.

The music has also appeared in more than 2,500 publicly observable Instagram Reels, with combined public social views exceeding seven million across recorded examples. These signals include audiences and visual contexts in Japan, Hong Kong, Brazil, and other regions.

日本語要約 Shazam検索は、どこかで曲を聴いた人が興味を持ち、自分で曲名を調べた行動です。3,500回以上のShazam検索に加え、公開確認できたInstagram Reelsは2,500件以上、公開SNS上の累計再生は700万回以上となっています。

Rights and licensing

Original music with a more direct path to permission

Nanashino-chan manages both the master recording rights and the composition rights for its original catalog. This can make rights review more straightforward than licensing music where several labels, publishers, composers, or other third parties must be contacted separately.

Commercial and sync licensing options are available for eligible creator videos, company-owned social content, client projects, sponsored content, brand videos, product demonstrations, AI-generated videos, and advertising campaigns.

The appropriate license depends on who is publishing the content, whether paid promotion is involved, the platforms used, the campaign duration, and whether the work is being delivered to a client.

権利管理について Nanashino-chanでは、オリジナルカタログの原盤権と楽曲著作権をプロジェクト側で管理しています。そのため、複数の第三者権利者が関与する楽曲と比較して、商用利用時の確認、記録、ライセンス手続きを比較的シンプルに進めやすい体制です。
