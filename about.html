Origin and Creative Direction

Nanashino-chan began YouTube activity in May 2024 and expanded into official digital music distribution in February 2025.

The project developed from a simple idea: background music can be quiet without becoming anonymous, and practical without losing its emotional identity.

The sound is inspired by Tokyo at night—quiet streets, late trains, soft lights, cafés, desks, rain, and the space between daily life and focused creative work.

Rather than competing with narration, captions, products, or visual storytelling, the music is intended to sit behind them. Soft drums, warm keys, understated melodies, and controlled arrangements help establish atmosphere while leaving room for the main message.

日本語：Nanashino-chanが目指しているのは、映像や言葉を支配する音楽ではなく、その背後で空気感、余韻、感情のトーンを支える音楽です。東京の夜や静かな日常をモチーフに、聴くための音楽と、映像の中で機能する音楽の両立を目指しています。

The Sound of Nanashino-chan

The Nanashino-chan catalog moves across lo-fi hip hop, chillhop, jazzhop, calm instrumental music, chillwave, and Tokyo-inspired night sounds.

The common thread is a low-distraction atmosphere with enough melody and texture to remain recognizable. Some tracks lean toward warm piano and jazz harmony, while others use relaxed grooves, city-night synths, soft percussion, or nostalgic textures.

This range allows the music to support focus, reading, late-night work, cafés, quiet listening, creator videos, product visuals, business communication, and other forms of digital storytelling.

Rights Management and Music Licensing

Nanashino-chan manages both the master recording rights and the composition rights for its original catalog.

This can make commercial rights review more straightforward than licensing music where several labels, publishers, composers, or other third parties must be contacted separately.

Commercial and sync licensing options are available for eligible creator videos, company-owned social content, client projects, sponsored content, brand videos, product demonstrations, AI-generated videos, and advertising campaigns.

The appropriate license depends on who is publishing the content, whether paid promotion is involved, the platforms used, the campaign duration, and whether the work is being delivered to a client.

日本語：Nanashino-chanでは、オリジナルカタログの原盤権と楽曲著作権をプロジェクト側で管理しています。企業動画、SNS、広告、AI動画、スポンサーコンテンツ、クライアントワークなどで使用する場合は、用途に合ったライセンスをご確認ください。
