What Is Jazzhop Lo-Fi Music?

Jazzhop is a subgenre of lo-fi hip hop that combines jazzy chords, live instrument textures, and boom-bap style beats. Often referred to as jazzy lo-fi or lofi jazz beats, this style creates a relaxed yet sophisticated sound.

Unlike standard lo-fi playlists, jazzhop music emphasizes musicality — including piano, saxophone, and subtle swing rhythms — making it ideal for both focused work and enjoyable background listening.
