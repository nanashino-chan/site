"Free to use" does NOT mean unrestricted use.

You can use the music for organic content creation only.

Allowed:

  • ✔ YouTube videos (including monetized)
  • ✔ TikTok / Instagram / Facebook posts (organic only)
  • ✔ Personal and creator content

Not allowed:

  • ❌ Paid advertising (YouTube Ads, TikTok Ads, Meta Ads)

For commercial campaigns or ads, please contact for licensing.
