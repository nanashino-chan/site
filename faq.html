Q15. License Pricing (How It Works)

Pricing depends on how the music is used.
Most projects fall under Commercial or Sync licensing.

If your content involves editing, branding, or any business-related purpose, a license is required.

1. Personal / Creator Use

For individual creators using music in organic, non-commercial content without editing.
Starting from $19 per track.

2. Commercial Use (Limited Cases)

Applies only to business-related content without editing or video integration.
$300 – $500 per project

Includes:
・Brand collaborations
・Sponsored or partnership content
・Business-related social media posts

If multiple accounts are involved, licensing applies per participating account.

Example:
・2 accounts → $500 × 2 = $1000
・3 accounts → $500 × 3 = $1500

Accounts directly involved in creation, editing, or publishing are considered participating accounts.
Tagging, mentions, or reposting alone are not included.

⚠️ If a project involves editing or video synchronization, it is classified as Sync use.

3. Sync License (Most Common)

Required for most professional use cases.
Starting from $500 – $1500+ per account

Applies when:
・Music is edited, cut, looped, or embedded
・Used in videos, ads, or branded content
・Synchronized with visual content

If a project involves both commercial use and editing, Sync licensing takes precedence.

💡 Quick Rule

If your content supports a business, brand, or audience growth,
it will require a Commercial or Sync license (most cases: Sync).
