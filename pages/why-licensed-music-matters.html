Why Licensed Music Matters

Many creators assume that purchasing music or finding music online automatically grants commercial usage rights.

However, modern platforms like YouTube, Instagram, TikTok, and Facebook use copyright detection systems that can affect monetization, visibility, and commercial usage.

Proper music licensing helps creators, brands, and agencies reduce legal and monetization risks while maintaining professional production standards.
