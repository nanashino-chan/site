Why Copyright Claims Happen

Platforms like YouTube and TikTok use automated systems to detect copyrighted music.
Even if you didn’t intend to violate rules, your video can still be flagged.

  • ⚠ Using unlicensed music
  • ⚠ Misunderstanding “royalty free”
  • ⚠ Reusing trending sounds without rights

What Happens If You Get a Claim?

  • ❌ Monetization disabled
  • ❌ Ads revenue goes to copyright owner
  • ❌ Video muted or blocked
  • ❌ Account restrictions (in severe cases)

一度のミスでも、収益やチャンネルに影響が出る可能性があります。

How to Avoid Copyright Claims

1. Use Licensed Music

The safest and most reliable way is to use music with a proper license.

2. Check Usage Rights Carefully

Not all “free” music allows commercial use or monetization.

3. Avoid Reuploading Popular Tracks

Trending songs are almost always protected by copyright systems.

The Safest Option for Creators

To completely avoid risks, use fully licensed, original music designed for content creators.
