Why Sponsored Videos Require Proper Music Licensing

Sponsored content is commercial content. Even if a creator purchased music personally or found “free music” online, additional licensing may still be required for monetized campaigns, paid partnerships, advertisements, and branded videos.

企業案件・スポンサー動画・PR案件では、 通常の個人利用とは異なる商用ライセンスが必要になる場合があります。

  • ⚠️ Content ID claims on sponsored uploads
  • ⚠️ Monetization conflicts
  • ⚠️ Brand campaign restrictions
  • ⚠️ Client approval issues
  • ⚠️ Copyright risks across social platforms

Platform Rules Still Apply to Sponsored Content

Major platforms including YouTube, Instagram, Facebook, and TikTok clearly state that purchasing or streaming music does not automatically grant commercial upload or redistribution rights.

ストリーミング楽曲の利用や、 「購入済み音源」の使用だけでは、 商用動画・広告案件・スポンサー投稿での利用許可にはならない場合があります。
