Which License Do These Tracks Need?

The track choice and the license choice are separate decisions. A track may fit your video creatively, but the correct license depends on how the video will be used.

Use Case Recommended License Notes
Personal non-commercial video Personal Use License For non-commercial, non-monetized, personal use only.
Monetized YouTube video Commercial Creator License For standard non-ad commercial online content.
Business website or organic social video Commercial Creator License For business use without paid ad campaign distribution.
Client project or agency deliverable Commercial / Sync / Custom License Depends on client, campaign, distribution, and advertising scope.
Paid social ad or brand campaign Sync License For ads, sponsored campaigns, promotional distribution, and brand use.
TV, cinema, games, apps, or exclusive use Custom License Request custom terms before production or publishing.

Platform Library and Credit-Based Usage Note

Some Nanashino-chan tracks may appear in social platform music libraries or user-generated content contexts. This can help with discovery, but platform-library usage and commercial licensing are not the same thing.

Credit-based or platform-library usage may be suitable for non-commercial or small-scale projects, but it should not be treated as a substitute for a commercial license, sync license, paid advertising license, client-work license, or brand campaign license.

Do not assume platform availability covers:

  • ✘ Paid ads
  • ✘ Brand campaigns
  • ✘ Client work
  • ✘ Business promotional videos
  • ✘ Sponsored content
  • ✘ External video editing and re-uploading
  • ✘ Using downloaded audio outside the platform
  • ✘ Sync licensing
  • ✘ Commercial campaign use

FAQ

What are the best Nanashino-chan tracks for commercial videos?

Recommended starting tracks include Smooth Groove for premium lifestyle and café visuals, Smooth Transitions for product demos and business videos, Stillness Vibes for creative work and calm visuals, Urban Twilight for stylish night scenes, and Smooth Beginnings for short cinematic cuts.

Can I use these recommended tracks in commercial videos?

Yes, if you purchase the correct license for your intended use. Standard non-ad commercial content may require a Commercial Creator License, while paid ads, brand campaigns, sponsored videos, and client advertising projects may require a Sync License.

Which track is recommended for brand videos?

Smooth Groove, Sunset Glow, Closing Time Jazz, Urban Twilight, and Smooth introduction are good starting points for brand videos, lifestyle visuals, café content, fashion content, and premium commercial video projects.

Which tracks are suitable for product demos or business videos?

Smooth Transitions, Stillness Vibes, Soft Sunsine, Calm Transitions, and Ambient Flow are suitable starting points for product demos, SaaS videos, business explainers, app demos, and creative workflow content.

Can I use Instagram or TikTok platform audio instead of buying a license?

Platform-library or credit-based usage may be suitable for non-commercial or small-scale projects, but it should not be treated as a substitute for a commercial license, sync license, paid advertising license, client-work license, or brand campaign license.

Do paid ads require a Sync License?

Paid ads, brand campaigns, sponsored campaign videos, client advertising projects, and broader promotional distribution generally require a Sync License or custom license.

Can I ask for track recommendations before buying?

Yes. For business videos, ads, AI videos, brand campaigns, product demos, and client work, you can contact Nanashino-chan with your project purpose, platform, video type, and desired mood.
