Why Commercial Music Licensing Matters

Using music in monetized videos, advertisements, sponsored content, or client work often requires commercial licensing permissions.

Many creators mistakenly assume streaming subscriptions or “free music” automatically include commercial usage rights. In reality, platforms may still apply copyright claims, monetization restrictions, or muted audio.
