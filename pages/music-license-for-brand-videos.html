Why Brand Videos Need Clear Music Permission

A brand video is not just a personal post with music. It helps shape the perception of a company, product, service, campaign, or commercial identity. Even when a brand video is posted organically on Instagram, TikTok, YouTube, LinkedIn, or a website, the purpose is still promotional or business-related.

That is why music for brand videos should be selected and licensed carefully. Streaming access, platform audio availability, or casual personal use does not automatically cover brand communication, client work, product promotion, campaign pages, or paid advertising.

日本語：ブランド動画は広告出稿していなくても、企業・商品・サービスの印象形成や販促に使われるため、商用利用として扱うべきです。 SNS上で使える音源と、ブランド動画で使える商用ライセンスは同じではありません。

Platform Audio Is Not Always a Brand Video License

A track may be available inside Instagram, TikTok, YouTube Shorts, or another social platform, but that does not automatically mean it is cleared for every brand video, paid ad, campaign asset, client deliverable, or long-term commercial use.

Platform-library or credit-based usage may be suitable for non-commercial or small-scale projects, but it should not be treated as a substitute for a commercial license, sync license, paid advertising license, client-work license, or brand campaign license.

日本語：SNS上で選べる音源として表示されていることと、ブランド動画・広告・クライアント案件で使えることは同じではありません。 商用目的の場合は、用途に合ったライセンス確認が必要です。

Simple Brand Video License Checklist

Before publishing a brand video with music, confirm the usage scope. This checklist helps decide whether commercial, sync, or custom licensing is needed.

  • Is the video representing a brand, product, company, service, or campaign? If yes, treat it as commercial use.
  • Is the video only an organic social post? A Commercial Creator License may be the starting point.
  • Will the video appear on a website, landing page, product page, or brand channel? Confirm commercial online usage scope.
  • Is the video sponsored or part of a paid partnership? Check whether commercial or sync licensing is needed.
  • Will the video be used as a paid ad or boosted post? Start from a Sync License or custom inquiry.
  • Is an agency or freelancer producing the video for a client? Make sure the client and project scope are covered.
  • Will the same music be used across multiple campaign assets? Confirm duration, platforms, territory, and media scope.

Important: A License Does Not Guarantee Platform Behavior

A music license clarifies permission from the rights holder. It does not guarantee how TikTok, Instagram, YouTube, Facebook, LinkedIn, advertising review systems, regional catalogs, account settings, automated audio systems, Content ID systems, or future platform policies will behave.

The safer approach is to use properly licensed music, keep documentation, choose the correct license, and check the platform rules before publishing or launching a campaign.

日本語：ライセンスは権利者からの使用許諾を明確にするものですが、 各プラットフォームの自動判定、広告審査、地域差、アカウント設定、将来の仕様変更まで保証するものではありません。

FAQ

Do brand videos need a music license?

In many cases, yes. Brand videos often support a company, product, service, campaign, or commercial identity. The correct license depends on whether the video is organic, sponsored, client-based, paid advertising, or broader campaign use.

What license do I need for a brand video?

Organic brand videos, website videos, product mood videos, and business videos may start from a Commercial Creator License. Paid ads, brand campaigns, sponsored campaigns, client advertising projects, and broader distribution may require a Sync License or custom permission.

Can I use platform audio for a brand video?

Platform-library or credit-based usage may be suitable for non-commercial or small-scale projects, but it should not be treated as a substitute for a commercial license, sync license, paid advertising license, client-work license, or brand campaign license.

Is a brand video the same as a paid ad?

Not always. A brand video may be an organic post, website video, or product mood video. A paid ad is distributed through advertising systems. Paid advertising and campaign use usually require broader licensing than organic brand content.

Can agencies use Nanashino-chan music in brand videos for clients?

Agencies may be able to license Nanashino-chan music for client brand videos, but the license should match the client, project, platforms, duration, campaign scope, and whether paid advertising is involved.
