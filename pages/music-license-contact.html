Why Licensing Matters

Many creators mistakenly believe that purchasing or streaming music automatically grants upload or monetization rights. However, major platforms officially state that commercial permissions may still require licensing agreements.

Proper licensing helps reduce risks related to:

  • ⚠ Content ID claims
  • ⚠ Monetization restrictions
  • ⚠ Copyright disputes
  • ⚠ Muted audio
  • ⚠ Sponsored content risks
  • ⚠ Client project complications

Official Platform Copyright Policies

Major platforms officially explain that purchasing or streaming music does not automatically grant upload, redistribution, monetization, or commercial usage rights.

  • YouTube Copyright Guidelines
  • Instagram Copyright Information
  • Facebook Copyright Policy
  • TikTok Copyright Policy
