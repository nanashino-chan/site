Why “Free Music” Is Not Safe

Many creators assume “copyright free” music is safe — but this is often not true.

  • ⚠ Copyright claims on YouTube
  • ⚠ Audio muted on TikTok / Instagram
  • ⚠ Content removal or demonetization

「フリー音源」でも、収益化や広告利用ではリスクがあります。

Official Platform Policies

Each platform has strict rules about music usage:

  • Instagram: Music usage guidelines
