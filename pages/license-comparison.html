Why Music Licensing Matters

Different music licenses allow different types of usage. Choosing the wrong license can lead to copyright claims, monetization problems, or restrictions on commercial projects.

音楽ライセンスには種類があり、 用途に応じて必要な権利範囲が異なります。

  • ⚠️ Content ID claims
  • ⚠️ Commercial use restrictions
  • ⚠️ Brand campaign limitations
  • ⚠️ Monetization risks
  • ⚠️ Client work complications

Understanding Different License Types

Personal License

Designed for non-commercial usage such as personal projects or hobby videos.

Commercial License

Supports monetized creator content, YouTube channels, and client projects.

Sync License

Required for advertisements, brand campaigns, films, and sponsored content.

Why Platforms Require Proper Licensing

YouTube, TikTok, Instagram, Facebook, and other platforms enforce copyright systems that detect copyrighted music automatically.

購入した音楽であっても、 アップロード権や広告利用権が 自動的に付与されるわけではありません。

  • ⚠️ Content ID detection
  • ⚠️ Monetization claims
  • ⚠️ Muted social media videos
  • ⚠️ Ad approval issues
  • ⚠️ Copyright disputes

Official Platform Copyright Policies

Frequently Asked Questions

Is royalty-free music always safe?

Not always. Some royalty-free music may still be registered in Content ID systems.

Do I need a sync license for ads?

Yes. Advertising, sponsored videos, and branded campaigns typically require commercial or sync licensing.

What license should YouTubers use?

Monetized creators generally need commercial licensing for safe usage.
