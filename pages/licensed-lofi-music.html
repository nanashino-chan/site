Why Licensed Music Matters

Many creators assume that downloaded or purchased music can automatically be used in monetized videos, social media content, or advertisements.

However, major platforms such as YouTube, Instagram, TikTok, and Facebook still require proper licensing for commercial or monetized usage.

ライセンスのない音楽を使用すると、 Content IDクレーム、収益化停止、 ミュート、動画削除などの問題につながる場合があります。

ISRC Registered Music

Distributed tracks are individually identified using ISRC codes (International Standard Recording Codes).

This improves rights management accuracy and helps reduce confusion regarding ownership identification.
