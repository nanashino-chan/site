Frequently Asked Questions

Can I use this music on YouTube?

Yes. Licensed tracks can be used in YouTube videos, Shorts, monetized content, and creator projects depending on the selected license plan.

Does purchasing music automatically grant commercial rights?

No. Purchasing or streaming music alone usually does not grant upload, monetization, advertising, or redistribution rights. Commercial licensing may still be required.

楽曲を購入しただけでは、 商用利用・広告利用・SNS投稿利用が自動的に許可されるわけではありません。

Can I monetize videos using this music?

Yes. Commercial licensing is designed for monetized creator content, branded videos, sponsored content, and business usage.

Is this music safe for YouTube Shorts, TikTok, and Instagram Reels?

Yes. The music is intended for modern creator platforms including YouTube Shorts, TikTok, Instagram Reels, podcasts, Twitch streams, and social media campaigns.

What is Content ID?

Content ID is YouTube’s automated copyright detection system. It scans uploaded videos and compares audio against registered music databases.

Improper music usage can result in copyright claims, monetization sharing, muted audio, or restrictions.

Is the music individually identifiable?

Yes. Distributed tracks are individually identifiable using ISRC codes, which help platforms and rights holders properly identify recordings.

配信楽曲にはISRCコードが付与されており、 音源単位で識別管理されています。

Can agencies and brands use this music?

Yes. Commercial licensing supports agencies, advertising campaigns, sponsored videos, corporate projects, and client productions.
