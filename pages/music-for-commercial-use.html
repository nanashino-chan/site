Why Commercial Licensing Matters

Many creators assume that purchasing music or finding “free music” automatically grants commercial rights. However, monetized videos, advertisements, and sponsored content often require proper licensing.

商用利用では、 「購入済み音楽」や「フリー音源」であっても、 別途ライセンスが必要になる場合があります。

  • ⚠️ Copyright claims
  • ⚠️ Demonetization risks
  • ⚠️ Brand campaign restrictions
  • ⚠️ Content removal or muted audio
  • ⚠️ Client work licensing problems

Official Platform Copyright Policies

Major platforms officially state that purchasing or streaming music does not automatically grant upload, monetization, advertising, or commercial usage rights.

  • YouTube Copyright Guidelines
  • Instagram Copyright Information
  • Facebook Copyright Policy
  • TikTok Copyright Policy

Copyright rules and platform policies may change over time. Always review the latest official guidelines before publishing monetized or commercial content.
