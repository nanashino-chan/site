Why Understanding Music Rights Matters

Many creators assume that purchasing music or streaming subscriptions automatically allow usage in YouTube videos, TikTok posts, advertisements, or monetized content. In reality, music rights are divided into multiple categories and often require specific licensing permissions.

音楽には複数の権利が存在し、 「購入した＝自由に使える」ではありません。 特にYouTubeやSNS、広告案件ではライセンス確認が重要になります。

Main Types of Music Rights

🎵 Master Rights

Rights related to the actual sound recording. Usually controlled by artists, labels, or distributors.

原盤権（録音された音源）

✍ Publishing Rights

Rights connected to composition, melody, lyrics, and songwriting.

著作権・作詞作曲権

🎬 Sync Rights

Permission required when synchronizing music with video content.

映像同期ライセンス

📡 Public Performance Rights

Rights related to broadcasting, streaming, and public playback.

演奏権・配信権

Why Copyright Claims Happen

Platforms like YouTube, TikTok, Instagram, and Facebook use automated copyright systems to detect copyrighted audio.

  • ⚠️ Content ID claims
  • ⚠️ Monetization redirected to another owner
  • ⚠️ Muted videos
  • ⚠️ Blocked uploads
  • ⚠️ Brand campaign risks

特に広告・案件動画では、 著作権問題が企業リスクになるケースもあります。

Official Platform Copyright Policies

Major platforms officially state that purchasing or streaming music does not automatically grant upload or commercial usage rights.

  • YouTube Copyright Guidelines
  • Instagram Copyright Information
  • Facebook Copyright Policy
  • TikTok Copyright Policy

ISRC Codes & Music Identification

Professionally distributed music is commonly assigned ISRC codes (International Standard Recording Codes).

ISRC codes help identify recordings individually across streaming platforms, distribution systems, licensing databases, and copyright management systems.

  • ✔️ Individual track identification
  • ✔️ Easier rights management
  • ✔️ Commercial licensing support
  • ✔️ Better tracking across platforms
  • ✔️ Reduced confusion between recordings

配信楽曲ごとに識別コードを管理することで、 権利管理やライセンス運用がより明確になります。
