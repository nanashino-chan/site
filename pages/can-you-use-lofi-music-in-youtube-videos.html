Lo-fi music is widely used in YouTube videos because it creates a calm atmosphere for studying, productivity, tutorials, podcasts, and lifestyle content.

Can You Legally Use LoFi Music on YouTube?

It depends on the licensing permissions associated with the music being used.

Some lo-fi tracks may only allow personal listening, while others may support creator workflows, monetized content, or commercial usage.

Creators should understand the difference between:

    • Personal listening rights
    • Upload permissions
    • Commercial licensing
    • Monetized content usage
    • Brand and sponsorship usage
    • Platform-specific copyright systems

Common Copyright Mistakes

Many creators incorrectly assume that:

  • Giving credit is always enough
  • Popular online tracks are free to use
  • "Royalty free" means unrestricted usage
  • Streaming music grants upload rights
  • Buying music automatically grants commercial permissions

YouTube copyright systems and platform policies may still apply depending on the music source and licensing structure.
