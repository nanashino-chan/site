What Is Music Licensing?

Music licensing is the general process of getting permission to use music legally.

  • ✔ YouTube videos
  • ✔ TikTok & Instagram content
  • ✔ Ads & commercial projects
  • ✔ Client work

What Is Sync Licensing?

Sync licensing (synchronization licensing) specifically means using music together with video content.

This is commonly used in:

  • 🎥 Films & documentaries
  • 📺 Commercial advertisements
  • 📱 Social media videos
  • 🏢 Corporate productions
