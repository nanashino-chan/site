Traditional Music Licensing Can Be Complicated

In traditional music licensing workflows, creators often need approval from multiple parties before using music commercially.

This may include:

  • ⚠ Contacting record labels for master use permission
  • ⚠ Requesting copyright clearance from organizations like JASRAC or NexTone
  • ⚠ Waiting weeks or even months for approval
  • ⚠ Paying multiple licensing and copyright fees

In many cases, licensing fees are calculated based on:

  • 📊 Audience size
  • 📺 Usage type
  • 🌍 Distribution territory
  • 💰 Commercial scale & campaign reach

For creators, brands, and businesses working on fast-moving content, traditional sync licensing workflows can become slow, expensive, and difficult to manage.
