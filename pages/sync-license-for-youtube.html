What Is a Sync License?

A sync license (synchronization license) allows music to be legally combined with video content. This includes YouTube videos, advertisements, short-form content, films, and branded campaigns.

音楽を映像と組み合わせて使用する際には、 Sync License（シンクライセンス）が必要になる場合があります。

  • ✔ YouTube videos
  • ✔ YouTube Shorts
  • ✔ TikTok & Instagram Reels
  • ✔ Commercial advertisements
  • ✔ Sponsored videos
  • ✔ Brand campaigns

Why Sync Licensing Matters on YouTube

Many creators assume purchasing music or streaming subscriptions automatically grants usage rights. However, platform copyright systems and copyright law may still require proper licensing.

特に収益化動画・広告案件・企業案件では、 適切なライセンス契約が重要になります。

According to YouTube’s official copyright guidelines , purchasing music alone does not automatically grant upload or commercial usage rights.

Proper licensing may still be required for monetized videos, sponsored content, and commercial distribution.

Official Platform Copyright Policies

Major platforms officially state that purchasing or streaming music does not automatically grant rights for uploading, monetization, commercial use, or social media distribution.
