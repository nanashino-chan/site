Personal Use License FAQ

Is the Personal Use License a commercial license?

No. It is limited to non-commercial, non-monetized personal use. It does not cover business use, client work, paid advertising, sponsored content, brand campaigns or sync licensing.

Can I use this license for a monetized YouTube video?

No. Monetized YouTube videos require a Commercial Creator License or another license appropriate to the project.

Can I use this license on Instagram, TikTok, Facebook or YouTube Shorts?

Yes, only when the post is a personal, non-commercial, non-monetized project within the license scope. Music selected from a platform's in-app library may also be subject to that platform's own rules.

Can I use this license for business videos, client work or advertising?

No. Business videos, client work, paid advertising, sponsored content, brand campaigns and promotional use require a commercial, sync or custom license.

Are audio files included with the Personal Use License?

No. This product grants a license only; no audio files are included.

What should I do if a Content ID or copyright claim appears?

If the project is within the permitted scope, contact Nanashino-chan with your Gumroad receipt, the track title or URL, the published content URL, the platform name and a screenshot of the claim when available.

What if I purchased the wrong license?

Contact Nanashino-chan before publishing. If the intended use is monetized, commercial, promotional, business-related or client-facing, purchase the appropriate higher license first.
