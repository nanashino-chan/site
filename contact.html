Important Licensing Notes

A music license gives permission to use Nanashino-chan music within the approved scope. It does not transfer ownership, exclusive rights, publishing rights, master ownership, or the right to resell or redistribute the music as standalone audio.

```
  • ✔ Keep your license receipt and any confirmation email as proof of license.
  • ✔ For sync licenses, track registration may be required before public use or campaign launch.
  • ✔ Content ID claim support is available for approved licensed use within scope.
  • ✔ Uses outside the license scope may require an upgrade or custom permission.
  • ✔ TV, film, cinema, games, apps, political advertising, and exclusive rights are not included in standard online licenses.
```

FAQ

```

Can I request a license for YouTube or TikTok?

Yes. You can request a license for YouTube videos, YouTube Shorts, TikTok videos, Instagram Reels, Facebook videos, LinkedIn videos, X posts, and other online content.

Can brands or agencies request a sync license?

Yes. Brands, agencies, marketers, editors, and client production teams can request sync licenses for ads, brand campaigns, product videos, paid social campaigns, sponsored content, and commercial online video projects.

Does a license guarantee that no Content ID claim will ever appear?

No. A license provides usage permission within the approved scope and supports claim resolution, but it should not be described as a guarantee that no automated claim will ever appear. If a claim appears, contact with your receipt, video URL, track title, and license confirmation.

Can I use the music in paid ads?

Paid advertising usually requires a sync license. Please include the campaign platform, usage period, brand or client name, and whether the ad will run on YouTube, Instagram, TikTok, Facebook, LinkedIn, X, or a website.

```
